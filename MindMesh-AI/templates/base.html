🧠 MindMesh AI

Transform Video Courses into an Intelligent AI Knowledge Base

What is MindMesh AI?

MindMesh AI converts educational videos into a searchable AI-powered knowledge system. Instead of watching hours of lectures, simply ask questions and receive precise answers with source references and timestamps.

How It Works

📹

Step 1: Upload Course Videos

Upload MP4 educational content.

🎙️

Step 2: AI Transcription

Faster-Whisper converts speech into structured text.

🧩

Step 3: Smart Chunking & Embeddings

Content is segmented and converted into semantic embeddings.

☁️

Step 4: Stored in Qdrant Cloud

Embeddings are indexed for lightning-fast retrieval.

🤖

Step 5: Ask Questions

Use Gemini or Groq to chat with your course content.

Example Questions

What is Flexbox?

Explain Python decorators.

Where was SQL JOIN explained?

What is Retrieval-Augmented Generation?

Summarize Chapter 3.

Key Features

  • ✅ AI-Powered Search
  • ✅ Video-to-Knowledge Conversion
  • ✅ Source Citations & Timestamps
  • ✅ Multi-LLM Support (Gemini + Groq)
  • ✅ Qdrant Semantic Search
  • ✅ Faster-Whisper Transcription

Important Note

MindMesh AI only answers based on uploaded course content, helping reduce hallucinations and improve answer accuracy.
